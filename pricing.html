FAQ

Common questions

Is early access really free?

Yes. During early access, the Starter plan is completely free with no credit card required. You'll be notified before any billing changes take effect.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the end of your billing cycle.

What counts as a query?

Each natural language question you ask counts as one query, including follow-ups in the same conversation. Viewing saved results or dashboards doesn't count.

Is my data stored by Talking 2 Data?

We never store your underlying data. We only hold encrypted connection credentials and, on Team plans, query metadata for audit logging.

Do you offer a free trial for paid plans?

Yes — Pro and Team plans include a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You get full access to every feature during the trial period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice (ACH or wire). Annual plans can be paid upfront for maximum savings.
